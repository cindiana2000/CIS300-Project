Our Services

Alarm Hawaii offers security services for residential and commercial customers. Our services are designed to help protect buildings, improve safety, and give customers more control over their property.

Surveillance Systems

Surveillance systems help customers monitor important areas of their home or business. Cameras can be placed near entrances, parking areas, hallways, offices, and other locations that need extra visibility.

Access Control

Access control systems help manage who can enter a building or restricted area. These systems are useful for businesses, offices, gates, storage rooms, and employee-only spaces.

Alarm Systems

Alarm systems help protect property by detecting activity and alerting customers when there may be a security concern. A reliable alarm system can improve safety and help customers respond faster.

Service Comparison

  • Surveillance systems help customers see what is happening around their property.
  • Access control systems help customers manage entry into buildings and rooms.
  • Alarm systems help alert customers when a security issue may be happening.
